About the Hackathon

About Us

Welcome, Innovators and Change-Makers!

SheBuilds Chennai Hack — Code & Challenge 1.0 is India's national-level, women-only 24-hour hackathon hosted at Rajalakshmi Engineering College on 1–2 August 2026. It brings women in engineering and technology together to learn, collaborate, and build innovative software solutions.

Whether you code every day or are just starting out — this is your space to build something real, collaborate with incredible women from across India, and showcase your ideas to industry leaders.

SheBuilds is a non-profit community with 12,000+ members, 500+ events hosted worldwide, and 70+ industry speakers and mentors. This is our biggest event yet.
